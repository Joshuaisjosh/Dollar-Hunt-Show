The FunFi Model

HOW IT WORKS

Three phases. One champion. A new era of crypto community engagement.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–2
P1

The Challenge

DHS posts challenge pillars on X based on the featured project. You comment or Quote Retweet with your most creative, knowledge-driven response. Your team scores entries. Top 20 advance.

Phase 2 · Week 3
P2

Community Vote

Top 20 appear on the DHS site split into two categories — Small Accounts (under 5k followers) and Large Accounts (5k+). Community votes once for one person. Top 5 from each category make the finals.

Phase 3 · Week 4
P3

Hot Seat Live

10 finalists compete live. Quick-fire round determines who earns the Hot Seat. 9 finalists guaranteed rewards. 1 wins the Hot Seat — the ultimate crypto knowledge crown.
